Food Waste and it's Management

Reducing food waste is important because producing food wastes energy and resources. Food that is not consumed contributes to climate change and wastes a lot of water.Food waste is a serious issue that can have negative effects on the environment and people's pockets


INTRODUCTION


Food waste is a major global problem that has negative effects on the environment, the economy, and society. It describes food that is lost, thrown out, or left uneaten despite being meant for human consumption.


WORLD FOOD PROBLEMS


World food problems encompass a range of interconnected challenges, from food insecurity and malnutrition to environmental degradation and inefficient distribution systems.


FOOD WASTE BY COUNTRY



Far too much food that is produced for human consumption goes to waste. While every country is facing their own battles against food waste, the scale of the problem varies from country to country..


SUSTAINABLE FOOD WASTE MANAGEMENT

Sustainable Management of Food is an approach that seeks to reduce wasted food and its associated impacts over the entire life cycle, starting with the use of natural resources ending with decisions on recovery or final disposal.
